Headphones are audio devices worn over the ears or inserted into the ear canal to listen to audio privately. They typically consist of two ear cups or earbuds connected by a band or wire. Headphones can be wired or wireless, utilizing Bluetooth or infrared technology for connectivity. They are used with various devices like smartphones, tablets, computers, and audio players to enjoy music, podcasts, calls, and other audio content without disturbing others nearby. Headphones vary in design, , offering different levels of comfort, sound quality, and noise isolation. Key features often include noise cancellation, microphone integration for calls, and controls for volume and playback.
